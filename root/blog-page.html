Get Rid Of Back Pain With These Asanas!

We all suffer from back pain at some point in our life. Some suffer less some more. Our back is the most ignored place in body. We hardly pay attention to it's health. In this blog we will learn how Yogasanas help you heal back pain.

1.Bhujangasana

The Bhujangasana as shown in above image is the best asana to cure back pain. It not only cures it but also prevents it to occur in future if done regularly.

Benefits:
  • It makes the spine supple.
  • Blood flow to back is increased.
  • Increased blood flow supplies more oxygern to muscles in the back.
  • The joints and ligaments become healthy too.
